Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026
TrainSync is an internal training records dashboard. We do not sell, share, or misuse your data in any way. This policy explains exactly what we access and why.

1. What We Access

When you sign in with Google, TrainSync requests access to:

  • Your basic Google profile — name and profile picture, used only to display your name in the dashboard header
  • Google Sheets (read & write) — used to read and write training records to a specific Google Sheet owned by your organisation

We do not access your Gmail, Google Drive files, contacts, calendar, or any other Google services.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Your name and photo are displayed in the dashboard UI only — they are never stored on any server
  • Your Google access token is stored in your browser's session storage and expires automatically when you close the tab
  • Training records you add are written directly to the organisation's Google Sheet — no third-party servers are involved

3. Data Storage

TrainSync has no backend server and no database of its own. All training data is stored exclusively in Google Sheets, which is governed by Google's own privacy policy and terms of service.

No personal data is stored on GitHub Pages or any other external server.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with any third parties. The only external service this app communicates with is Google's own APIs (accounts.google.com and sheets.googleapis.com).

5. Cookies & Tracking

TrainSync does not use cookies. It does not track your usage, behaviour, or location. There are no analytics, advertisements, or tracking scripts of any kind.

6. Your Rights

You can sign out at any time using the Sign out button, which immediately clears your session. You can also revoke TrainSync's access to your Google account at any time by visiting myaccount.google.com/permissions.
